Blessings stack **multiplicatively** as modifiers on the deity-granted **power** they belong to (not as standalone passive buffs).

This card was rewritten on 2026-05-14 to reflect the new combat-design pass; the old "Ishtar +25% damage, Hathor +1 HP regen" passive-blessing model is replaced. See [[Blessing system]] for the canonical description.

## Stacking model
- Each god grants one power + one blessing.
- The blessing is a multiplicative modifier on that god's power (e.g. +20% chain lightning bounces).
- Multiple instances of the same blessing on the same power compound multiplicatively (1.2 × 1.2 × 1.2 …), producing the late-run "juiced" state the design wants.

## Run-shape implications
- The early run is a search for blessings that match the powers you brought.
- The mid-run is about hitting two or more stacks on a single power.
- The late run is the multiplicative cliff — a power can become several times stronger than its base form.
