15 Runescape-shaped skills, each pair of which raise one of the 5 attributes. Combat AND crafting both legitimately progress the character.

## The 15 skills
| Attribute | Combat skill | Crafting skills |
|---|---|---|
| Stamina | Defence | Cooking, Construction |
| Strength | Strength | Smithing, Mining |
| Agility | Attack | Fishing, Hunter |
| Intellect | Magic | Herblore, Crafting |
| Spirit | **Devotion** | Farming, Foraging |

- **No Prayer skill.** Replaced by **Devotion** — gains XP when powers are cast. Functions as the "active caster" skill that ultimately raises Spirit.
- RS's other ~8 skills (Fletching, Woodcutting, Firemaking, Thieving, etc.) are dropped or folded.

## XP → attribute conversion (milestone-based)
- Each skill caps at **level 99** (RS-faithful).
- **Every 10 skill levels = +1 to the fed attribute.**
- A skill at level 99 contributes +9 to its attribute.
- An attribute fed by 3 skills, all at 99: +27 contribution.

## Skill effects on combat
Skill level affects combat in **two ways**:
1. **Stat bonus** via its attribute (per milestones above).
2. **Direct AP-cost reduction** on the related action (higher Attack skill = cheaper swings; higher Magic skill = cheaper power casts).

## Design intent: parallel power-up paths
- Combat-only player: Strength + Defence + Attack + Magic + Devotion grind attributes.
- Crafting-only player: Smithing + Mining + Cooking + Construction + Fishing + Hunter + Herblore + Crafting + Farming + Foraging grind the **same** attributes.
- A "Stardew day" in the oasis is legitimate character progression.
