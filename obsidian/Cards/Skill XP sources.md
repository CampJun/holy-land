Author the per-skill XP rules — what activity grants which skill XP, and how much.

## Why this is a card
Runescape's clarity comes partly from its crisp skill-XP rules: cook food at a fire → Cooking XP; mine a rock → Mining XP; cast a spell → Magic XP. Each Holy Land skill needs the equivalent.

## Combat skills
| Skill | XP source |
|---|---|
| Attack | Successful melee hit (per swing) |
| Strength | Damage dealt with melee |
| Defence | Damage taken or attack dodged |
| Magic | Power cast |
| Devotion | Power cast (small parallel XP — different from Magic; Magic is the action, Devotion is the deity-attunement) |

## Crafting skills (Strength)
| Skill | XP source |
|---|---|
| Smithing | Smelting + smithing items at forge |
| Mining | Striking ore nodes |

## Crafting skills (Stamina)
| Skill | XP source |
|---|---|
| Cooking | Cooking raw food at hearth/fire |
| Construction | Building/upgrading oasis structures |

## Crafting skills (Agility)
| Skill | XP source |
|---|---|
| Fishing | Catching fish (per catch) |
| Hunter | Killing animals (non-demon) or setting traps |

## Crafting skills (Intellect)
| Skill | XP source |
|---|---|
| Herblore | Identifying herbs + brewing potions |
| Crafting | Making non-metal items (leather, jewelry, etc.) |

## Crafting skills (Spirit)
| Skill | XP source |
|---|---|
| Farming | Planting + harvesting crops |
| Foraging | Picking wild plants / scouting biomes |

## XP curve
RS uses an exponential curve (level N → level N+1 XP). Adopt or adapt? TBD.

## Open
- Whether Magic and Devotion both gain XP from a single cast (double-dip) or split (each cast = small Magic + small Devotion).
- XP-per-action magnitudes — needs balancing math.
- Whether failing actions grants partial XP (RS gives 0 for failed cooks; Holy Land could differ).
