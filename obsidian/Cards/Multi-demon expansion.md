> Add 1–2 more demons + 1–2 more blessings to start surfacing combo space.

Each new demon wants:
- Distinct glyph + palette so they read instantly on the CP437 grid.
- AI variation — the imp's plain chase isn't the whole vocabulary. Candidates: ranged, fleeing-when-low-HP, summons-on-death, charges-in-straight-lines, splits-on-hit.
- A loot table flavored to its origin (demon-specific drops, scaling currency).
- A biome bias once cultural ruins land — Egyptian-flavored demons in Egyptian ruins, etc. Pair design with [[Cultural ruin biomes]].

## Stat-block tier
Per the 2026-05-14 combat-design pass, **enemies are tiered** (see [[Enemy tiered stat blocks]]):
- New demon types in this card are **grunt-tier** — simplified block (HP, damage range, AP, move cost, abilities), no full attribute layer.
- Boss-tier demons (full player-style block + powers) are out of scope for this card; they come later and reuse the boss schema.

Couples tightly to [[Blessing combo and stacking]] because the value of more blessings is having more demon archetypes to combo against.
